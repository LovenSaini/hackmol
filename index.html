*Rescue and Redistribution*

Prioritize rescuing surplus food from various sources such as restaurants, grocery stores, and farms, and redistribute it to those in need through partnerships with food banks, shelters, and community organizations.

Learn More

*Raise Awareness and Advocacy*

Educate the public about the environmental, social, and economic impacts of food waste through awareness campaigns, while advocating for policy changes at local, national, and international levels to address the root causes of food waste and hunger.

Learn More

*Community Engagement and Empowerment*

Engage and empower individuals and communities to take action against food waste by providing education, resources, and practical tips on reducing waste at home, as well as facilitating community-based initiatives such as food-sharing networks and community gardens.

Learn More

*Sustainable Solutions and Innovation*

Collaborate with stakeholders across the food supply chain to develop and implement sustainable solutions for reducing food waste, including the adoption of innovative technologies, practices, and policies that promote efficiency, conservation, and resilience in food systems.
